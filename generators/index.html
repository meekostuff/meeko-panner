Supported generators

Most generators of web documentation-sets rely on framesets or frames. These typically have primary content in a frame, with banner, navigation, etc in the top window or in other frames.

This site design is actually a good indication that the desire for a content-first approach has been around for a long time. The downfall of doing this with frames is the lack of flexibility with regards to layout and, especially, the inability to easily bookmark or hyperlink to individual pages.

meeko-panner allows these sites to be bookmarkable by assuming the primary content is in the top window and adding the banner and navigation into the same window with AJAX.
